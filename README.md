**Mufetti** is an online social media application designed for music enthusiasts, creators, and artists. Through its intuitive interface, it allows users to **create posts sharing their original songs**, **covers**, or **thoughts on music**. These posts include engagement features such as **reactions** and **comments**, allowing the community to interact with shared content.  

Users can discover content through a **global feed** or by **searching for specific albums and artists**, with the ability to import data directly from **MusicBrainz**. Authenticated users have a **customized profile** featuring an avatar and stats like **followers and following counts**. To further personalize the experience, users can **follow others** to prioritize **relevant content in their recommendations**.  

The platform encourages connection through specialized **Groups**, where users with shared musical interests can interact. All interactions are supported by a **notification system** to keep users updated on community activity. Mufetti also prioritizes privacy, allowing users to **control access to their profiles and posts** at any time.
